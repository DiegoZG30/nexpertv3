COMMON QUESTIONS

Most Popular Questions

What services does N-Expert.ai provide?

N-Expert.ai provides AI consulting, custom AI agent development, Multi-Agent System (MAS) implementation, workflow automation, and ongoing support. We specialize in making AI practical and profitable for small and midsize businesses.

How long does it take to deploy an AI agent?

Typical deployment takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple conversational agents can be ready in as little as 1 week, while complex Multi-Agent Systems may take 6-8 weeks.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll pro-rate the difference and help you transition smoothly to your new tier with zero downtime.

What integrations are supported?

We support 200+ integrations through n8n including CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot), messaging (WhatsApp, Slack, Teams), email, calendars, databases, and custom APIs.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a free 30-minute consultation and demo of our AI agents. For qualified businesses, we provide a 14-day pilot program to test specific use cases.

How do you ensure data security?

We follow enterprise-grade security practices including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, role-based access control, and data residency options. Your data never leaves your approved infrastructure.

What kind of support is included?

All plans include onboarding support. Starter includes email support, Professional adds priority response times, and Enterprise gets a dedicated account manager with 24/7 availability.

Can I customize my AI agents?

Yes! Every AI agent is customized to your brand voice, business processes, and specific requirements. We train agents on your data and continuously optimize their performance.

What industries do you serve?

We serve professional services, legal firms, real estate, construction, insurance, and healthcare among others. Our agents are designed to adapt to industry-specific regulations and workflows.

What is JurisMind™?

JurisMind™ is our proprietary RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) platform that gives AI agents access to verified legal knowledge bases, enabling accurate legal research, contract analysis, and compliance checking.
