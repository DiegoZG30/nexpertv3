About Us

Meet the team behind N-Expert.ai and our mission to make AI accessible to every business

WHO WE ARE

Driven by Purpose. Backed by Expertise.

Our mission goes beyond technology. We help organizations adapt AI responsibly, strategically, and effectively — guided by real-world experience and deep domain knowledge. N-Expert.ai was founded to bridge the gap between enterprise AI capabilities and the small and midsize businesses that need them most.

Our Mission

Make agentic AI accessible to small and midsize businesses, helping them work smarter, grow faster, and increase profitability.

Our Vision

Become the most trusted partner in making agentic AI practical and impactful for SMBs in the U.S. and Latin America.

Our Capabilities

Design and integrate multi-agent AI systems that automate workflows, enhance engagement, and support smarter business decisions.

OUR LEADERSHIP

The People Behind N-Expert.ai

Decades of experience in technology, business, and community service — united by a shared vision for accessible AI.

Francisco Vega Jr.

President, Founder & CEO

Seasoned entrepreneur with 35+ years serving the DC-Metro Hispanic market. Founded Vega Hispanic Yellow Pages in 1985 and led it until its 2004 sale. Transitioned to digital with Local Digital Works and VEGA DIGITAL. Co-founded N-Expert.ai in 2024 to bring AI automation to small businesses. Passionate about serving the Hispanic community and a dedicated supporter of the Intégrate Campaign (Volunteer Fairfax).

J. Walter Vera

Vice President

35+ years of strategic consulting and technical implementation across U.S. government agencies including NASA, USCIS, IRS, and DOD. Graduate in Math, Physics & Systems Engineering. Led technology labs and SDLC projects. Contributed to the INS redesign and USCIS-ICE integration under the 2003 Homeland Security Act. Brings innovation and federal-grade technical expertise to N-Expert.ai.
